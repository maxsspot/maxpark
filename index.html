About us

MaxPark is a park created by two friends at Adams Ricci Park. All the rides are free and we are always working to add more rides. Look at the information below to learn about the rides.



The Swingster

Come to a ride of twists and pushes! The estimated ride time is around 1 minute. Want to ride again? Just get back in line! But if there's no line, feel free to keep riding.



TBS

Get pushed and pulled on this ride. Ride time is around one minute. Want to get on again? Just get back in line! But if there's no line you could ride again!
